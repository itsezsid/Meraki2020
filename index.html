Welcome to Meraki 2020!

Literature goes beyond books

November 12th

About Meraki 2020

Everybody’s first thought when someone says literature is dusty books and even dustier statues. But the world of literature and language encompasses a vibrant and widespread variety of things that go beyond just Shakespeare and Dickens. And this is exactly what Meraki 2020 is all about. So join us for a day filled with events, games and competitions that celebrate literature for what it truly is - diverse, spirited and most of all - FUN.

Timeline of Meraki 2020

Juniors (6 - 8)

  • 8:00am - 8:30am

    Veritum Veritas (Inauguration and Slam Poetry)

    Since its inception, poetry has remained a powerful creative medium used to talk implicitly about explicit issues. With the student poetry circle becoming more visible than ever, the locus of change is shifting to a younger audience. And on November 12th, this powerful platform will serve to hoist up your views on the unbridled chaos we call the contemporary world. So if you have something to say and the beat to perform it to, then this is the event for you.

  • 8:30am - 9:15am

    Once Upon A Time

    For all the mythology and folklore lovers - this one’s for you! Pick up your favourite story and bring it to life in this epic storytelling competition. All you have to do is leave your listeners at the edge of their seats!

  • 9:30am - 10:30am

    Whodunit

    Think you are the next Sherlock Holmes? Find out by solving this classic Whodunnit case full of puzzles and riddles that will surely make you put your thinking caps on!

  • 10:30am - 11:15am

    Back to the Future

    The Back to the Future event is all about bringing your favorite elements of science fiction to life! This can be done in any form; ranging from blueprints to full-scale models. Along with this, we have a debate on a topic related to a theme which is widely discussed in science fiction literature.

  • 11:15am - 12:15pm

    Draw-A-Toon

    Lovers of comics, manga, or webtoons can show off their artistic talent by creating a newspaper-style comic in one hour with a positive message embedded into it.

  • 12:45pm - 1:45pm

    The Poetic Escapade

    Calling all budding poets to harness their creativity. A poem on an unseen and predefined picture and prompt has to be produced within a given time limit. May the best poet win!

  • 2pm - 3pm

    The Laugh Lounge

    Everybody thinks they’re the funniest person in the room. This is your chance to prove it. Grab a mike and a chair and try your hand at something we all know and love - standup comedy

Seniors (9 - 12)

  • 8:00am - 8:30am

    Veritum Veritas (Inauguration and Slam Poetry)

    Since its inception, poetry has remained a powerful creative medium used to talk implicitly about explicit issues. With the student poetry circle becoming more visible than ever, the locus of change is shifting to a younger audience. And on November 12th, this powerful platform will serve to hoist up your views on the unbridled chaos we call the contemporary world. So if you have something to say and the beat to perform it to, then this is the event for you.

  • 8:30am - 9:15am

    Historical Debate

    Every time history repeats itself; the price goes up. And when the stakes are this high, everyone wants to weigh in on what they think went right and what went wrong. History was written by the winners but this is your chance to paint a different picture. This turncoat debate gives you a unique opportunity to notice the voice of the vanquished along with the victor’s version - and allow the audience to come to their own conclusions. With controversy and hypocrisy plaquing every page of history, this is an even you don't want to miss.

  • 9:15am - 9:30am

    Blood Between the Pages

    From cryptic puzzles to gory murder, this next event is as close as you’d get to solving a real crime from the comfort of the bed you’ve been tucked up in all quarantine. It’s a thrilling and day-long murder mystery designed to bring out the inner detective you think you have. With new, real time updates at every corner this event will keep you on your toes. The only thing simple about it are the rules - first to solve the case wins. So, grab your coat, cap and cigar for the game is afoot.

  • 9:30am - 10:30am

    Classics

    Quiz on the Classics
    This is the ultimate test on classic literature. From Homer to Shakespeare, Greece to Broadway, this live quiz will test everything you know about literature's greats. You better bring your A game - history has its eyes on you.

    Paints and Poets
    Every literary event is incomplete without the one aspect that makes it all come alive - art. Join us for a unique event where you draw, paint or even doodle your interpretation of a piece of literature. It's all in your head, now put it on paper.

  • 10:30am - 11:15pm

    Comic-verse

    A comic book trivia game that brings the thrill and excitement of a game show to your doorstep. Jump into the world of one of literature's most underrated genres with Gautam Madhu - your ever-charming host. If you're a comic book nerd, this is an event you cannot miss!

  • 11:15pm - 12:15pm

    Nirvana - Live Writing

    Sometimes all it takes to lift the mood is your favourite song. And sometimes all it takes is a beautifully-worded poem. With this event, we weave the two together to give you one of the most unique events of the day. The objective is to write a poem on the spot about a song of your choice but a theme of ours. So put your headphones on and get a quill because this event is the best of both worlds.

  • 12:15pm - 12:45pm

    Lunch + Crime Update 1

  • 12:45pm - 1:45pm

    Sci-fi

    Live Sci-Fi themed Storywriting Call upon your wildest imaginations and scientific knowledge to create an amazing sci-fi story from the given writing prompt. Most unique and brain-boggling story wins.
    Word Limit- 500 to 600 words

  • 1:45pm - 2:00pm

    Break + Crime Update 2

  • 2:00pm - 3:00pm

    Stand Up Comedy + Closing Ceremony

    Tickle your funny bone and join the stand up comedy event, featuring live virtual comic performances by participants!
